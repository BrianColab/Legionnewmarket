# PRODUCT.md — Branch 426 Royal Canadian Legion, Newmarket

## Product Purpose
Public-facing website for Royal Canadian Legion Branch 426 in Newmarket, Ontario. Serves as the branch's digital home — informing veterans, members, and the community about services, events, hall rentals, and membership. Content is static; no logins, no dynamic data.

## Users
- Veterans and their families seeking information about benefits and programs
- Current and prospective members
- Community members interested in events or hall rentals
- Local general public searching for the Legion address, phone, hours

## Register
brand

## Brand
Institution: RCL Branch 426, Newmarket, ON (est. 1946). Tone is dignified, welcoming, community-rooted. Never corporate. Never playful. The poppy and service are the emotional anchors. Respect, remembrance, belonging.

## Anti-References
- Generic nonprofit templates (pale blue + white + stock photos)
- Government site aesthetic (heavy, bureaucratic)
- Sports club / bar vibe
- Overly festive / cheerful designs

## Strategic Principles
- Legibility first — many users are older
- Honour the institution — dark, bold design signals weight and seriousness
- Red (legion red) is sacred; use it sparingly as a true accent
- Spacing and typography carry the hierarchy; avoid decoration
