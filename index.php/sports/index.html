Sports

The Royal Canadian Legion Zone E2 Sports Program for 2024-2025

Eligible players are Legion Members:  Life, Ordinary, Associate & Affiliate (all must be in good standing).  Membership must be paid by January 31, 2025.  Membership cards must be produced.

All entries must include Teams, Doubles and Singles on the most current form only.

All entries must be on a Provincial Sports form submitted to the Branch Sports Officer.  The entries must be signed by the Branch President and Branch Membership Chairman.

No one is allowed to leave a tournament without the permission of the convenor.

Below is a list of sports events.  Please go to the Branch for further information/details:

* Cribbage Tournament at Newmarket Legion on Sat Nov 30  - Branch entry closing date Nov 8

* Darts Tournament at Sutton Legion on Sat Dec 7 - Branch entry closing date Nov 9

* 8 Ball Tournament at Stouffville Legion on Sat Jan 11 - Branch entry closing date Dec 13

* Seniors Dart Tournament at Innisfil Legion on Sat Jan 25 - Branch entry closing date Jan 3

* Euchre Tournament at Aurora Legion on Sat Feb 8 - Branch entry closing date Jan 17

* Shuffleboard Tournament at Mount Albert Legion on Sat Feb 22 - Branch closing date Jan 31

* Mixed Darts Tournament at Stouffville Legion on Sat Mar 22 - Branch entry closing date Feb 26

* Washer Toss Tournament at Aurora Legion on Sat May 3 - Branch entry closing date Apr 11

* Horseshoe Tournament at Sutton West Legion on Sat May 17 - Branch entry closing date Apr 24
