Veterans

Any Veteran needing assistance by Veterans Affairs Canada can call toll free: English 1-866-522-2122; French 1-866-522-2022. Click on this link to be connected with Veterans Affairs Canada website directly.
 
All Canadian Armed Forces Veterans may now apply for the new Veterans Service Card. Please click on the following link - Veterans Service Card.
 
ONTARIO IS GIVING BACK. Free fishing for Veterans and active Members Canadian Armed Forces members. As of January 1, 2019 all Veterans and active Canadian Armed Forces members residing in Ontario can enjoy free recreational fishing. For more information visit www.ontario.ca/fishing or call 1-800-667-1940.

Ontario Provincial Parks.  As of Nov 6, 2023 - Free weekday use for Veterans and active Canadian Armed Forces members.  Please click here for more information and booking  

Veterans and members of the Canadian Armed Forces can ride for free on GO Transit year round when travelling with their Veteran’s Service Card or a Canadian Military Service ID beginning 1 March 2025. This includes regular and reserve force members.     Please click on this link to be connected to GO Transit for further information

Wheels for the Wise provides private non-emergency medical transportation services for medical appointments.  VAC cards accepted for Veterans with direct billing to Medavie Blue Cross.  Please click here for more info 

Ontario Command would like to advise Branches, Veterans and Family Members of Veterans, that it has sponsored the MDSC PATSS peer support online workshops that are being offered with new training dates of  Apr 28-29 and May 12-13.  This workshop is free offering to Veterans and Family Members.    Please click here for more information and registration
